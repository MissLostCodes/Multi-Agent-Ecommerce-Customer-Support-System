# Returns & Refunds Policy

## Standard Returns
Customers may return items within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging.

## Final Sale Items
Items marked as "final sale" are not eligible for return or refund under any circumstances.

## Hygiene Products
Hygiene items (e.g., personal care, undergarments) cannot be returned once opened.

## Perishable Goods
Perishable goods (food, flowers, groceries) are non-returnable after delivery.

## Exception: Damaged or Late Delivery
If perishable goods arrive damaged or spoiled due to delayed delivery:
- Customer may be eligible for partial or full refund
- Evidence (photo) required

## Opened Items
Opened non-hygiene items may be eligible only if defective.

## Refund Method
Refunds are issued to original payment method within 5–7 business days.
